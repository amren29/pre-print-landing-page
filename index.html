PDF preflight reports

Generate downloadable PDF reports of all preflight checks for any job. Share with customers or keep for your records.

Email notifications

Automatic email alerts when customers upload artwork, when checks complete, and when proofs are approved or rejected.

Custom upload pages

Branded upload pages for your customers. Add your shop logo and colors. Mobile friendly and works on any device.

Re-upload workflow

Failed files? Customers fix and re-upload against the same job. No new link needed, version history tracked.

Job history and tracking
